RELEASE 1.0
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Influence Counter is a influence or life counter for the TCG The Spoils and similar games.

Influence Counter is primarly a tool for the trading card game (TCG) The Spoils, and
can be used to keep track of one or both players Influence during a game.

It can also be used to keep track of life in games like Magic
the Gathering, Yo-Gi-Oh! and similar games.


---------------------------------
USAGE
---------------------------------

- Swipe left and right to switch between 1 player and 2 player mode.
- Press above the counter to increase it.
- Press below the counter to decrease it.

- Pressing the option button will open a configuration page with options to 
  customize starting values as well as other options.
